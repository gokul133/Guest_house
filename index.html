Hostel Office

At IIITDM Kancheepuram, the operation of the dormitories and dining halls is overseen and coordinated by the Hostel Office, a separate administrative division. To ensure that the students may benefit fully from their time at university, the hostel office strives to offer a peaceful, secure, and safe environment.
